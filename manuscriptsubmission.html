Manuscript Submission

Prospective authors are invited to electronically submit regular papers of their work in English. Submit the full paper using the following layout. Limitation to 6 full pages, including paper title, authors and affiliations, figures, and references (maximum size 2MB). Up to 2 additional pages are allowed, each additional page incurs a fee of $ 150 per page and a full paper can have a maximum of 8 pages including the additional pages. Authors are requested to prepare the manuscripts in the IEEE two-column format template and to translate it in PDF. No other format in the submission and in the final version can be accepted. The IEEE two-column format template can be downloaded from this link.
